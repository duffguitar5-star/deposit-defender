\## 1. ABSOLUTELY PROHIBITED LANGUAGE



The application MUST NOT use language that:



\### Implies legal advice or conclusions

\- “You are entitled to”

\- “You have a legal right to”

\- “The landlord violated the law”

\- “This is illegal / unlawful”

\- “The landlord must”

\- “You should sue”

\- “You should file a claim”

\- “You can recover damages”

\- “This guarantees a refund”



\### Implies legal strategy or pressure

\- “Demand letter”

\- “Final notice”

\- “Failure to comply”

\- “Consequences”

\- “Penalties”

\- “Statutory damages”

\- “Bad faith”

\- “Liable for”



\### Implies representation or advocacy

\- “On your behalf”

\- “We assert”

\- “We argue”

\- “We advise”

\- “We represent”



---



\## 2. REQUIRED DISCLAIMERS (CONCEPTUAL)



All user-facing surfaces MUST clearly convey:



\- This service is NOT a law firm

\- No legal advice is provided

\- This is a document preparation and informational service only

\- No outcome is guaranteed

\- Users may wish to consult a licensed Texas attorney



Exact wording may vary, but meaning MUST remain intact.



---



\## 3. APPROVED SAFE LANGUAGE PATTERNS



\### Informational framing

\- “According to publicly available information…”

\- “In general, Texas rules describe…”

\- “This information is provided for general awareness only…”

\- “Based on the information you entered…”



\### Factual statements (user-provided)

\- “The tenant states that…”

\- “The deposit amount listed was…”

\- “The move-out date provided was…”



\### Neutral references

\- “Publicly available Texas guidance commonly references…”

\- “General timelines often mentioned include…”



---



\## 4. DOCUMENT TONE REQUIREMENTS



All generated documents must be:

\- Neutral

\- Professional

\- Non-accusatory

\- Non-threatening

\- Informational in tone



Avoid emotional, adversarial, or coercive language.



---



\## 5. IF UNCERTAIN



If something cannot be expressed safely:

\- Omit it

\- Use high-level informational wording

\- Recommend consulting a Texas attorney (without elaboration)



Safety overrides completeness.



---



\## 6. CHANGE CONTROL



Any change to this file requires explicit approval from the project owner.
