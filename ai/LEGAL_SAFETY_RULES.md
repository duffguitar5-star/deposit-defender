\# LEGAL\_SAFETY\_RULES.md



\## 1. NON-LEGAL SERVICE LIMITATION (ABSOLUTE)



This product is STRICTLY a non-legal, non-attorney service.



The service provides ONLY:

\- Document preparation

\- Information organization

\- High-level summaries of publicly available rules

\- Formatting of user-supplied facts into professional documents



The service MUST NOT:

\- Provide legal advice

\- Provide legal opinions

\- Provide legal strategy

\- Interpret facts as legal conclusions

\- Predict legal outcomes

\- Recommend actions such as suing, threatening litigation, or negotiating

\- Represent users in any dispute

\- Communicate with landlords, courts, or attorneys on behalf of users



The service is NOT a law firm and does NOT create an attorney-client relationship.



---



\## 2. TEXAS-ONLY SCOPE



All content, copy, logic, and references MUST be limited to the state of Texas.



The service MUST NOT:

\- Mention or support any other state (including Florida)

\- Present multi-state logic

\- Offer state selection

\- Compare Texas law to other states



If a rule or citation is not Texas-specific, it must be framed as:

“General informational context only, not legal advice.”



---



\## 3. SAFE LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS



All generated language MUST:

\- Use neutral, informational phrasing

\- Avoid directive or advisory verbs (e.g., “should,” “must,” “you are entitled to
